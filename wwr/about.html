History

Rafting Radical was created by two friends whom are lovers of radical sports in 1990. Bruno and Josemar were companions in activities like surf,rock climbing and rafting since the high school.

A hobby that evolved to a lifestyle. 34 years of pure adrenalin: our work, our life, our passion.
